Common HR Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why should we hire you?

Communication & Soft Skills

  • Practice confident self-introduction.

  • Participate in mock HR roleplays.

Interview Tips for Success

Research the company before appearing for the interview.
Dress professionally and maintain eye contact.
Always carry a copy of your resume and portfolio.
Be honest about your experience and skills.
Ask insightful questions about the company or role.
Practice mock interviews with a timer for realism.
